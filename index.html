The Fascinating History of the Yo-Yo

The yo-yo is one of the oldest toys in human history, with origins tracing back to ancient China and ancient Greece. This remarkable spinning disc has fascinated people for thousands of years. The modern yo-yo as we know it became popularized in the Philippines during the 1920s and was later introduced to America.
